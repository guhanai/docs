**Settings is everything about your workspace itself — name, teammates, sender accounts, billing, integrations. Everyone-in-one-place plumbing.**

Open from the gear icon at the bottom of the sidebar.

## General

- **Workspace name** — how your workspace shows in the sidebar and on invites
- **Workspace slug** — the URL fragment (e.g., `guhan.ai/acme` where "acme" is the slug)
- **Time zone** — used for sending windows, sweep schedules, and Meetings display
- **Delete workspace** — permanent; contact support to restore

## Members

Manage who has access.

- **Invite** — send an email invite; pick a role
- **Change role** — promote / demote a teammate
- **Remove** — offboard a teammate (their sender accounts stay but need reassignment)
- **Pending invites** — resend or revoke

## Roles

Beyond the 3 built-in roles (Workspace Admin, Campaign Manager, Sales Rep), you can create custom roles with granular permissions.

Built-ins:
- **Workspace Admin** — full access
- **Campaign Manager** — create + edit + launch agents; can't delete workspace records or manage billing
- **Sales Rep** — see + reply to assigned prospects only

See [Roles and permissions](/workspace-settings/roles-and-permissions) for details.

## Sender accounts

Every LinkedIn / email / WhatsApp account Guhan uses to send.

- **+ Connect** — add a new sender
- **Reauth** — re-authorize when OAuth expires
- **Schedule** — set the sender's active hours + timezone + weekdays
- **Quotas** — adjust per-action daily caps
- **Owner** — reassign to a teammate
- **Disconnect** — remove; pauses any in-flight sequences using this sender

See [Sender accounts](/get-started/sender-accounts/overview) for concept + safety detail.

## Calendars

Connect your calendar for meeting bookings.

- **Google Calendar** — native OAuth
- **Outlook Calendar** — native OAuth
- **Cal.com** — paste your booking URL
- **Calendly** — paste your booking URL

Configure meeting duration, buffer time, meeting title template, available hours.

See [Meetings](/meetings/overview) for the full flow.

## Do Not Contact (DNC)

The workspace-wide block list.

- **Emails** — specific email addresses to never message
- **LinkedIn URLs** — specific people to never message
- **Domains** — every prospect at this domain gets auto-blocked (add your own domain here so you don't outreach yourself)
- **Companies** — LinkedIn company URLs; auto-blocks all their employees

Enforced globally across every agent.

## Data sources

Guhan's data pipelines that power discovery, enrichment, and sender OAuth. All fully managed — nothing for you to configure.

- **Discovery** — the pipelines that find prospects matching your ICP + buying signals
- **Enrichment** — verified email + phone reveal on demand
- **Sender OAuth** — the secure connection layer for your LinkedIn, Email, and WhatsApp senders

You'll see connection status here. There's nothing to buy separately — everything is included in your Guhan plan.

## Billing

Manage your plan + credits + payment method.

- **Current plan** — Starter / Growth / Scale + current billing cycle
- **Credit balance** — monthly grant + top-up pool + last 50 ledger entries
- **Buy credit pack** — top up with a 200 / 1,000 / 2,000 credit pack
- **Change plan** — upgrade (immediate) or downgrade (at cycle end)
- **Payment methods** — cards + billing address
- **Invoices** — download past invoices
- **Cancel subscription** — takes effect at cycle end

See [Plans and credits](/workspace-settings/plans-and-credits) for the full pricing overview.

## Notifications

Configure how Guhan pings you.

- **Real-time** — new Inbox task, meeting request, sender reauth needed
- **Daily digest** — 8am summary
- **Weekly summary** — Monday morning stats
- **Channels** — email / in-app / SMS / WhatsApp (if you enabled Ask Guhan on WhatsApp)

For programmatic access — currently limited to a few internal use cases. Contact hello@guhan.ai if you need API access.
