By the end of this you'll have Guhan finding prospects for you every day and reaching out to them in your voice.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- A LinkedIn account (personal or Sales Navigator both work)
- Your company website URL
- 10 minutes

<Note>
  Guhan starts you on a **7-day free trial**. No credit card required to try it. You get 500 credits per month on the Starter plan.
</Note>

## Step 1 — Sign up

Go to [guhan.ai](https://guhan.ai) and click **Get started**. Give us your email + password + name, verify your email, and you're in.

## Step 2 — Connect LinkedIn

Guhan will ask you to connect LinkedIn. Click the button, sign in with LinkedIn, and come back.

<Tip>
  Guhan uses your LinkedIn to **find prospects** (searching for people who match your customer) AND to **send outreach** (invites, DMs, comments on their posts). One connection covers both.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Confirm your brand

Guhan reads your website + LinkedIn profile and drafts your **brand summary**:

- What you sell
- Who buys it
- What problems you solve
- How you'd like your messages to sound

Read it. Edit anything that doesn't match how you'd say it.

<Warning>
  This is the most important step. Everything Guhan writes to prospects is based on this summary. Spend 60 seconds getting it right.
</Warning>

## Step 4 — Describe your ideal customer

Two quick questions:

**Who are they?**
- Their job titles (e.g., "VP of Sales", "Head of Marketing")
- Where they're based (city, country, or region)

**What kind of company?**
- Industry (e.g., "SaaS", "Fintech")
- Company size (e.g., 50–500 employees)
- Where the company is headquartered

<Tip>
  You don't need to list every job-title variation. Type "VP of Sales" once and Guhan automatically matches "Vice President Sales", "SVP Sales", "Head of Sales", etc.
</Tip>

## Step 5 — Choose your triggers (optional)

Triggers tell Guhan **when** to reach out to someone.

Pick one or more:

- 💰 **They raised funding** recently
- 📈 **They're hiring** for roles you sell to
- 🎯 **They engaged with a competitor's post** on LinkedIn
- 📢 **They posted about a topic** you care about
- 👔 **A new leader joined** in a relevant seat

You can also skip this step — Guhan will just find people matching your ICP with no trigger required.

## Step 6 — Preview 5 prospects

Guhan runs a real LinkedIn search and shows you 5 people who match your criteria. Look at each one:

- Right person? Keep them.
- Wrong person? Reject them.

Your accepts and rejects help Guhan tune the search before it launches for real.

## Step 7 — Launch

Click **Launch**. Guhan starts working:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finds prospects">
    Within a few minutes, real prospects who match your criteria land in your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Writes messages">
    For each prospect, Guhan reads their profile and writes a personalized message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sends the sequence">
    Default sequence: send a LinkedIn invite → wait 3 days → send a DM. You can rewrite this anytime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waits for replies">
    When a prospect replies, Guhan drops a task in your Inbox so you can jump in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
