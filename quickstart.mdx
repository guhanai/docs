By the end of this you'll have Guhan finding prospects for you every day and reaching out to them in your voice.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- Your **company website URL** (Guhan reads it to understand what you sell)
- A **LinkedIn account** (you'll connect this after launch, not during setup)
- 10 minutes

<Note>
  Guhan starts you on a **7-day free trial**. No credit card required to try it. You get 500 credits per month on the Starter plan.
</Note>

## Step 1 — Sign up

Go to [guhan.ai](https://guhan.ai) and click **Get started**. Give us your email + password + name, verify your email, and you're in.

## Step 2 — Paste your website URL

The onboarding wizard starts by asking for your **company website URL**. Paste it and click **Continue**.

Guhan fetches your site in the background — reads your homepage, about page, and product content — and uses that to prefill everything else in the wizard.

<Tip>
  If you followed a "Find prospects" CTA from our landing page, your website URL is prefilled automatically. One click and you're moving.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Confirm your brand

Guhan drafts your **brand summary** based on what it read from your site:

- **Company name**
- **Industry** — LinkedIn's industry taxonomy
- **Company description + value proposition** — 2-3 sentences on what you sell
- **Customer pain points** — the specific problems your customers face
- **Preferred tone** — professional / casual / friendly / direct / playful

Every field pre-filled by Guhan gets an **✨ AI-generated** pill. Read each line — edit anything that doesn't sound like you.

<Warning>
  This is the most important step. Every message Guhan writes to prospects references this summary. Spend 60 seconds getting it right.
</Warning>

## Step 4 — Describe your audience

This step spans two quick screens grouped as **"Define your audience"**:

### Who to reach

- **Job titles** — the roles you sell to (e.g., "VP of Sales", "Head of Marketing")
- **Location** — where they're based (city, country, or region)

<Tip>
  You don't need to list every job-title variation. Type "VP of Sales" and Guhan auto-generates matches for "Vice President Sales", "SVP Sales", "Head of Sales", etc.
</Tip>

### What companies

- **Industry** — pick from LinkedIn's list (multiple OK)
- **Company size** — headcount range
- **Exclusions** (optional) — specific companies or keywords to skip

## Step 5 — Preview 5 prospects

Guhan runs a real search and shows you 5 people who match your ICP. For each one:

- **Accept** — this is exactly the kind of person I want
- **Reject** — no, this isn't right

Your accepts and rejects tune the search before launch. If you reject most of them, Guhan refines and shows you fresh candidates.

## Step 6 — Launch

The final screen shows you a summary of what Guhan will do. Before you launch:

- **Add buying signals** (optional) — click the "Buying triggers" card → pick signals like "just raised funding" or "engaged with a competitor's post"
- **Pick your sender channel** — inline card → LinkedIn / email / WhatsApp (or connect it later)

Then click **Launch**. Guhan starts working:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finds prospects">
    Within a few minutes, real prospects who match your criteria land in your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Writes messages">
    For each prospect, Guhan reads their profile and writes a personalized message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sends the sequence">
    Default sequence: LinkedIn invite → wait 3 days → LinkedIn DM. You can rewrite this anytime.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waits for replies">
    When a prospect replies, Guhan drops a task in your Inbox so you can jump in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 7 — Connect LinkedIn (post-launch)

If you skipped connecting a sender during onboarding, your Outreach Agent will show an inline banner: **"Connect LinkedIn to start sending"**. Click it, complete OAuth, and messages start going out.

You can also add email + WhatsApp senders anytime from Settings.
