**By the end of this page, Guhan will be finding real prospects for you every day and reaching out to them in your voice — on the channels you choose (LinkedIn, Email, WhatsApp, or all three).**

No credit card. No sales call. Just your website URL and 10 minutes.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- **Your company website URL** — the one input Guhan really needs. It reads your site to understand what you sell.
- **At least one channel to send from** — a LinkedIn account, a Gmail/Outlook email address, or WhatsApp. You'll connect it either on the launch screen or right after launch. You can add more later.
- **10 minutes.**

<Note>
  Free for 7 days. No card required. Starter plan = 500 credits/month — plenty to see Guhan work.
</Note>

## The 5 setup steps

Setup is 5 short screens. Sender connect (LinkedIn / Email / WhatsApp) happens either **inline on the launch screen** or **right after launch** — not as a wizard step.

## Step 1 — Sign up + paste your website URL

Go to [guhan.ai](https://guhan.ai). Click **Get started**. Enter email + password + name, verify your email, and you're in.

The very first onboarding screen asks for your **company website URL**. Paste it and hit **Continue**.

**What Guhan does behind the scenes:** fetches your homepage, about page, and product content in the background. It uses the content to prefill everything else in the wizard.

**What to think about:**
- Use your main marketing site, not a subdomain or landing page.
- If you have multiple products, use the site that best represents your main offering.
- Asset-heavy sites (large images, WordPress, JavaScript-rendered pages) all work fine.

<Tip>
  If you came in from the "Find prospects" box on our landing page, your URL is already prefilled. One click and you're moving.
</Tip>

## Step 2 — Confirm what Guhan learned about you

Guhan drafts a **brand summary** from your website:

- **Company name**
- **Industry** — mapped to LinkedIn's official categories
- **What you sell + why it matters** — 2-3 sentences
- **What problem your customers face** — the pain you solve
- **Preferred tone** — professional, casual, friendly, direct, or playful

Every AI-drafted field gets an **✨ AI-generated** pill. Read every line. Fix anything that doesn't sound like you.

<Warning>
  **This step matters more than any other.** Every future message Guhan writes to prospects is anchored on this summary — the same summary powers messages sent via LinkedIn, Email, and WhatsApp. Spend a real minute here.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  You can revisit this anytime under **Brand & Voice** in the sidebar. Nothing is locked.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Describe your ideal customer

Two quick screens grouped as **"Define your audience"**:

### Who to reach

- **Job titles** — the roles you sell to ("VP of Sales", "Head of Marketing").
- **Location** — city, country, or region.

<Tip>
  Don't overthink this. Type "VP of Sales" and Guhan auto-adds variations like "Vice President Sales", "SVP Sales", "Head of Sales".
</Tip>

### What companies

- **Industry** — pick from LinkedIn's list (multiple is fine).
- **Company size** — headcount range.
- **Anyone to skip** (optional) — specific companies or keywords.

<Note>
  Buying signals (funding, hiring, engagement) are NOT captured here — they live on the launch screen as an optional inline popup. See Step 5.
</Note>

## Step 4 — Review 5 real prospects

Guhan runs a real search and shows you 5 people who fit what you just described. For each:

- **Accept** — yes, this is my kind of buyer
- **Reject** — no, this isn't right

Your yes/no tunes the search before you launch. Reject a bunch and Guhan pulls fresh candidates (up to 3 cycles).

<Tip>
  Look at each prospect's current role, company, and headline. Would you actually send them a personalized message? Yes → accept. No → reject.
</Tip>

## Step 5 — Launch

The final screen shows a summary of what Guhan is about to do. Two optional inline cards before you click **Launch**:

### Buying triggers (signals) — inline popup

Click the **Buying triggers** card → opens a popup with signal picker:
- 💰 Funding raised recently
- 📈 They're hiring
- 👔 New leadership joined
- 🎯 Engaged with a competitor's post
- 📢 Posted about specific keywords
- 🎁 Engaged with a specific post URL

Pick zero, one, or several. Skip entirely to run a plain ICP search.

### How to reach them — inline popup

Click the **How to reach them** card → opens a popup with three channel options. Pick one, several, or skip entirely:

- **LinkedIn** — connect via OAuth. Highest volume + reply-quality mix.
- **Email (Gmail / Outlook)** — connect via OAuth. Large daily volume, native inbox threading, calendar integration.
- **WhatsApp** — QR scan with your phone (personal or business WhatsApp). Highest reply rate; lower daily volume.

You can connect any channel now, all three, or none — a "Connect \[channel\] to start sending" banner appears on your Outreach Agent after launch if you skip.

### Click Launch

Guhan starts working:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Guhan enrolls your accepted prospects">
    The 5 people you accepted in Step 4 are your seed prospects — first ones in your List, no waiting for the first sweep.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan activates your Watchlist Agent">
    Starts finding more prospects matching your ICP + signals. First scheduled sweep fires within 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan creates your Outreach Agent">
    Bound to a default 3-step sequence: LinkedIn invite → wait 3 days → LinkedIn DM. You can swap in Email or WhatsApp steps any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan starts drafting messages">
    Within 5-10 minutes, the first personalized messages are ready. If a sender is connected, they queue to send during your active hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After launch

You land on the **Outreach Agent detail page**. From here:

- **Prospects tab** — see who Guhan enrolled + who's in-flight.
- **Sequence tab** — rewrite the default flow. Add Email or WhatsApp steps.
- **Sources tab** — see + edit the Watchlist Agent that feeds this.
- **Settings tab** — name, product, senders, handoff behavior.

If you didn't connect any sender at launch, an inline banner shows: **"Connect a channel to start sending"**. One click, OAuth, done. Add more channels anytime from Settings.
