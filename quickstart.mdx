**By the end of this page, Guhan will be finding real prospects for you every day and writing them personal messages in your voice.**

No credit card. No sales call. Just your website URL and 10 minutes.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- **Your company website URL** — this is the one input Guhan really needs. It reads your site to understand what you sell.
- **A LinkedIn account** — you'll connect this after setup, once you can see what Guhan plans to send.
- **10 minutes.**

<Note>
  Free for 7 days. No card. You get 500 credits/month on the Starter plan — plenty to see Guhan work.
</Note>

## Step 1 — Sign up

Go to [guhan.ai](https://guhan.ai) and click **Get started**. Enter email + password + name, verify your email, and you're in.

## Step 2 — Paste your website URL

The very first onboarding screen asks for your **company website URL**. Paste it and hit **Continue**.

In the background, Guhan is already reading your homepage, about page, and product content — pulling out everything it needs to write in your voice.

<Tip>
  If you came in from the "Find prospects" box on our landing page, your URL is already filled in. One click and you're moving.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Confirm what Guhan learned about you

Guhan drafts a **brand summary** from your website:

- **Company name**
- **Industry** — mapped to LinkedIn's official categories
- **What you sell + why it matters** — 2-3 sentences
- **What problem your customers face** — the pain you solve
- **Preferred tone** — professional, casual, friendly, direct, or playful

Every AI-drafted field gets an **✨ AI-generated** pill. Read every line. Fix anything that doesn't sound like you.

<Warning>
  **This step matters more than any other.** Every future message Guhan writes to prospects is anchored on this summary. Spend a real minute here.
</Warning>

## Step 4 — Describe your ideal customer

Two quick screens grouped as **"Define your audience"**:

### Who to reach

- **Job titles** — the roles you sell to ("VP of Sales", "Head of Marketing")
- **Location** — city, country, or region

<Tip>
  Don't overthink this. Type "VP of Sales" and Guhan auto-adds variations like "Vice President Sales", "SVP Sales", "Head of Sales".
</Tip>

### What companies

- **Industry** — pick from LinkedIn's list (multiple is fine)
- **Company size** — headcount range
- **Anyone to skip** (optional) — specific companies or keywords

## Step 5 — Review 5 real prospects

Guhan runs a real LinkedIn search and shows you 5 people who fit what you just described. For each one:

- **Accept** — yes, this is my kind of buyer
- **Reject** — no, this isn't right

Your yes/no tunes the search before you launch. Reject a bunch and Guhan pulls fresh candidates.

## Step 6 — Launch

Last screen. You see a summary of what Guhan is about to do. Before you launch:

- **Add buying signals** (optional) — click the "Buying triggers" card → pick signals like "just raised funding" or "engaged with a competitor's post". Signals mean Guhan only reaches people who look ready.
- **Pick your first sender channel** — LinkedIn / Email / WhatsApp (or connect it right after launch).

Click **Launch**. Guhan is now working:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Guhan finds prospects">
    Within a few minutes, real people who match your criteria start showing up in your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan writes messages">
    For each prospect, Guhan reads their profile and writes a message specific to them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan runs the sequence">
    Default: LinkedIn invite → wait 3 days → LinkedIn DM. You can change this any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guhan handles replies">
    When someone actually replies, Guhan drops a task in your Inbox so you can jump in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 7 — Connect LinkedIn (right after launch)

If you skipped connecting a sender during onboarding, your Outreach Agent shows a banner: **"Connect LinkedIn to start sending"**. Click it, sign in through LinkedIn's own screen, and messages start going out.

Add Email + WhatsApp senders anytime from Settings.
