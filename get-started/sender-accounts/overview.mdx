Guhan doesn't send messages from a Guhan email or a Guhan LinkedIn account — it sends from **your** accounts. You connect them once, Guhan uses them for you.

## Which senders you can connect

  <Card title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Email (Gmail / Outlook)" icon="envelope">

## How to connect one

Go to **Settings → Sender accounts → Connect**. Pick a provider, sign in with that provider, and Guhan handles the OAuth handshake.

<Note>
  Guhan uses [Unipile](https://www.unipile.com) as the underlying provider for LinkedIn / Gmail / Outlook / WhatsApp. Your credentials go through their secure OAuth flow — Guhan never sees your password.
</Note>

## One account, dual role

Your LinkedIn sender account plays **two roles**:

1. **Sending** — invites, DMs, comments to prospects
2. **Discovery** — searching for prospects, pulling reactor lists off posts

Both count toward the same daily quota. Guhan balances discovery vs. sends automatically — if you're near your daily send cap, discovery slows down to protect sends.

## Daily limits

LinkedIn's published safe caps:

| Action | Default | Adjustable up to |
|---|---|---|
| Invites | 25/day | ~40/day |
| DMs | 30/day | ~50/day |
| Profile views | 30/day | ~500/day |
| Comments | 100/week | 100/week |
| Reactions | 300/week | 500/week |

For email + WhatsApp:

| Provider | Default | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Gmail | 100/day | Google's soft cap for personal accounts |
| Outlook | 100/day | Microsoft's soft cap |
| WhatsApp | 40/day | Unipile personal-app tier |

You can adjust these per-account in **Settings → Sender accounts → [account] → Quotas**.

## Warm-up

New sender accounts (< 30 days old) start at reduced capacity and ramp over time:

- **Day 0–7**: 30% of full pace
- **Day 8–14**: 50%
- **Day 15–21**: 75%
- **Day 22–30**: 90%
- **Day 30+**: 100%

Established accounts (existing LinkedIn history, > 150 connections) skip the warm-up entirely.

## Sending window

Every sender has an active **sending window** — the hours + days of the week when messages actually go out. Default: 10am–6pm Mon–Sat, in the sender's timezone.

Set narrow windows to look more human. Set broader windows to send more per day. Guhan spreads sends randomly within the window.

## Multiple senders on one Outreach Agent

You can attach multiple senders to a single Outreach Agent — Guhan will rotate through them to spread volume. Useful when you have 2-3 LinkedIn accounts (yours + team members') feeding the same campaign.

## What happens if a sender breaks

If your LinkedIn account gets restricted, or you change your Google password, Guhan can't send anymore. You'll see:

- A **banner at the top of the app** — "Your LinkedIn account needs re-authentication"
- **In-flight sequence steps** hold in place until you fix it
- **New enrollments** pause until at least one working sender exists

Click the banner or go to Settings → Sender accounts → **Reauth** to fix.
