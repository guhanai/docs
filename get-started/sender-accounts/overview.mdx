title: Sender accounts
description: The LinkedIn, email, and WhatsApp accounts Guhan uses to reach prospects.

**A sender account is one of your own accounts — LinkedIn, Gmail, Outlook, or WhatsApp — that Guhan uses to send messages on your behalf.**

Guhan never messages from a Guhan address. It sends from you. You connect the account once, Guhan uses it for you.

<Note>
  **Sender accounts are only for sending.** Finding prospects is done entirely by Guhan's own data pipelines — your personal accounts are never touched to search for people. If you only want to see prospects Guhan finds (without messaging), you don't need to connect any sender at all.
</Note>

## Which senders you can connect

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin">
    Personal LinkedIn or Sales Navigator. Sends invites, DMs, comments, profile views, reactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email (Gmail / Outlook)" icon="envelope">
    Standard Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 accounts. Sends outbound + threads inbound replies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="WhatsApp" icon="whatsapp">
    Personal or business WhatsApp. Sends outbound + captures inbound replies.
  </Card>

## How to connect one

Go to **Settings → Sender accounts → Connect**. Pick a channel, sign in with that provider through their official OAuth screen, and Guhan handles the handshake.

<Note>
  Guhan never sees your password. All connections go through the provider's own secure OAuth flow.
</Note>

## Daily limits

LinkedIn's published safe caps:

| Action | Default | Adjustable up to |
|---|---|---|
| Invites | 25/day | ~40/day |
| DMs | 30/day | ~50/day |
| Profile views | 30/day | ~500/day |
| Comments | 100/week | 100/week |
| Reactions | 300/week | 500/week |

For email + WhatsApp:

| Provider | Default | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Gmail | 500/day | Well inside Google's provider-side ceiling |
| Outlook | 500/day | Well inside Microsoft's provider-side ceiling |
| WhatsApp | 40/day | Personal-app tier (safe operating range 30-50/day) |

You can adjust these per-account in **Settings → Sender accounts → [account] → Quotas**.

## Warm-up

New sender accounts (< 30 days old) start at reduced capacity and ramp over time:

- **Day 0–7**: 30% of full pace
- **Day 8–14**: 50%
- **Day 15–21**: 75%
- **Day 22–30**: 90%
- **Day 30+**: 100%

Established accounts (existing LinkedIn history, > 150 connections) skip the warm-up entirely.

## Sending window

Every sender has an active **sending window** — the hours + days of the week when messages actually go out. Default: 10am–6pm Mon–Sat, in the sender's timezone.

Set narrow windows to look more human. Set broader windows to send more per day. Guhan spreads sends randomly within the window.

## Multiple senders on one Outreach Agent

You can attach multiple senders to a single Outreach Agent — Guhan will rotate through them to spread volume. Useful when you have 2-3 LinkedIn accounts (yours + team members') feeding the same campaign.

## What happens if a sender breaks

If your LinkedIn account gets restricted, or you change your Google password, Guhan can't send anymore. You'll see:

- A **banner at the top of the app** — "Your LinkedIn account needs re-authentication"
- **In-flight sequence steps** hold in place until you fix it
- **New enrollments** pause until at least one working sender exists

Click the banner or go to Settings → Sender accounts → **Reauth** to fix.
