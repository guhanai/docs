title: Connect WhatsApp
description: Add WhatsApp as a sender for prospect outreach or Ask Guhan on your phone.
---

WhatsApp is the highest-reply-rate channel Guhan supports — prospects treat WhatsApp as personal, not marketing. Volume is lower (40 messages/day per account) but engagement per message is much higher.

WhatsApp also unlocks **Ask Guhan on your phone** — chat with your workspace from your phone by messaging Guhan on WhatsApp.

## Two use cases

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Outreach sender" icon="paper-plane">
    Guhan sends WhatsApp messages to prospects as part of a sequence.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ask Guhan on the go" icon="mobile">
    Chat with your workspace from your phone — "show me today's replies" via WhatsApp.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The connection flow is the same for both — you connect once, then decide what the sender does.

## What you'll need

- Your phone with **WhatsApp installed** (personal WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business, both work)
- **60 seconds** to scan a QR code

<Note>
  Guhan uses [Unipile](https://www.unipile.com) as the WhatsApp integration provider. Same secure OAuth substrate as LinkedIn and email — nothing goes through Meta's Cloud API (no template pre-approval, no Business Manager setup).
</Note>

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Sender accounts → + Connect">
    Sidebar → Settings → Sender accounts. Click **+ Connect** in the top-right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick WhatsApp">
    From the provider grid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code with your phone">
    A QR code appears in the browser. On your phone:

    - **WhatsApp** → Settings → **Linked Devices** → **Link a Device**
    - Scan the QR code on screen

    Same flow as connecting WhatsApp Web.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    Once scanned, your browser shows "Connected — verifying…" for 5-10 seconds. Then the sender appears in your list with an **Active** dot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what this WhatsApp account does">
    Two toggles on the sender's row:
    - **Use for outreach** — Guhan can use this WhatsApp for outbound sequence steps
    - **Use for Ask Guhan** — WhatsApp becomes a chat interface for your workspace (message "@Guhan show me today's replies" from your phone)

    You can enable both.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily limits

WhatsApp has the tightest safety limits of any channel:

| Setting | Default | Adjustable up to |
|---|---|---|
| Messages per day | 40 | 100 |
| Messages per hour | 10 | 15 |

<Warning>
  WhatsApp doesn't use Meta's Cloud API (no template pre-approval required) but your personal/business account can still get temporarily banned if you send high-volume outreach. Stick close to defaults.
</Warning>

## What Guhan sends

Every WhatsApp message is:
- **Generated per prospect** — no template blasts
- **Sent from your name + your WhatsApp number**
- **A real 1:1 conversation** — prospects reply back to you directly

Guhan reads incoming replies + drops them in your Inbox like any other channel.

## Prospect must have a phone

WhatsApp requires the prospect to have a phone number. If Guhan doesn't have their phone yet:
- Guhan reveals it at send time via a phone lookup (costs 12 credits)
- OR skips this step if the reveal fails (no phone number found)

Enable phone reveal on your workspace's Auto-reveal settings if you want Guhan to proactively look up phones as prospects land.

## Ask Guhan on WhatsApp

If you enabled **"Use for Ask Guhan"**, you can chat with your workspace from your phone:

```
You:   @Guhan show me today's replies
Guhan: [message thread]

You:   @Guhan how many prospects landed today?
Guhan: 8 prospects landed today across 2 Watchlist Agents. Want the breakdown?
```

Same commands as the web chat — see [Ask Guhan concept](/ask-guhan) for the full list.

<Tip>
  The `@Guhan` prefix is required. Without it, messages you send from your phone are treated as normal WhatsApp messages (they go to the person or group you're messaging).
</Tip>

## Disconnecting

Settings → Sender accounts → [WhatsApp account] → **Disconnect**.

This:
- Immediately stops all outreach through this WhatsApp
- Ends the Ask-Guhan-on-WhatsApp session
- Doesn't affect your WhatsApp app itself — just removes Guhan's linked-device session

You can also unlink from your phone: WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices → Guhan → Log out.

## Reauth

If WhatsApp's linked-device session expires (usually after 30 days of inactivity), Guhan can't send.

- Banner at the top of the app: "Your WhatsApp account needs to reconnect"
- Settings → Sender accounts → [account] → **Reconnect** → scan QR again

Takes 30 seconds.

## What if the QR code times out?

QR codes expire after 60 seconds. If yours does:
- Click **Regenerate QR** in the modal
- Scan the fresh one
