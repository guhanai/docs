**Guhan is one AI sales agent split into two jobs.** One finds people worth reaching out to. The other actually reaches out. Together they run outbound for you.

## The two agents

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Watchlist Agent" icon="binoculars">
    **Finds prospects.** Searches Guhan's data pipelines every day for people who match your buyer AND show signals they're ready.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outreach Agent" icon="paper-plane">
    **Reaches out.** Reads each prospect's profile, writes a personal message, runs the full follow-up.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

They pass work between each other:

```
Watchlist Agent → finds people → drops them into a List → Outreach Agent picks them up → sends personal messages
```

## The 5 things Guhan actually does

This is the whole loop — same one shown on our landing page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Detect buying signals">
    Guhan scans its data pipelines continuously — new hires, funding rounds, tech-stack changes, post engagement — to find who's actually ready to buy right now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Research every prospect">
    Before writing anything, Guhan reads that person's profile, role, company, and recent activity. So the message references what actually matters to them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrich and verify contact info">
    Pulls verified email, LinkedIn URL, phone number, company data — in seconds. No spreadsheets, no lookup tools, no guessing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reach out personally">
    Writes and sends hyper-personal messages across LinkedIn, Email, and WhatsApp — one at a time, each shaped to that prospect. Never a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book the meeting">
    Handles the follow-ups. Reads replies. Converts warm interest into a real meeting on your calendar — while you focus on closing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## A concrete example

Say you sell an analytics tool to VPs of Marketing at SaaS companies.

**Your Watchlist Agent** (you set this up once):
- Look for: **VP of Marketing** at **SaaS companies** with **50-500 employees**
- Only reach out when: they **posted about attribution** in the last 30 days

Every day, the Watchlist Agent runs. It finds people matching that description and drops them into your **List**.

**Your Outreach Agent** (also set up once):
- Pulls prospects from that List
- Runs this sequence:
  - Day 0 — LinkedIn invite
  - Day 3 — LinkedIn DM
  - Day 7 — Email
  - Day 14 — Follow-up email

Every message is written from scratch, referencing the specific post that person made about attribution.

**You** open Guhan once a day, see who replied, jump into the interesting conversations. That's it.

## The work you never see

You don't have to think about any of this — but it's why Guhan actually works:

- **Reads every profile before writing** — so the message quotes their real role, company, recent post.
- **Throttles daily volume** to LinkedIn-safe limits — so your account doesn't get restricted.
- **Retries failed sends** — rate limits, network blips, temporary bans — so you don't lose anyone.
- **Withdraws stale invites after 14 days** — keeps your pending queue clean.
- **Classifies every reply** — meeting requests turn into calendar links, out-of-office replies pause the sequence for 7 days, unsubscribe requests drop them onto Do Not Contact.

## Where you fit in

Guhan handles the volume. You handle the judgment:

- **Review prospects** the Watchlist Agent found — is this really your buyer?
- **Approve drafts** when Guhan asks for a second look on a high-stakes reply.
- **Take over threads** — Guhan hands you the conversation, you close it.
