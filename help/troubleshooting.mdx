## Sender accounts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="LinkedIn sender says 'needs re-authentication'">
    Your LinkedIn OAuth token expired (usually happens if you changed your password or logged out from all sessions on LinkedIn).

    **Fix:** Settings → Sender accounts → [account] → **Reauth**. One-click re-sign-in with LinkedIn.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="LinkedIn account got restricted">
    LinkedIn flagged unusual activity and paused your account temporarily.

    **Fix:**
    1. Sign in to LinkedIn manually (not through Guhan) — you'll see a message from LinkedIn
    2. Appeal or wait out the restriction (usually 24-48 hours for a first offense)
    3. Once LinkedIn lifts it, return to Guhan → Settings → Sender accounts → Reauth
    4. Guhan resumes at 50% of pace to help rebuild reputation

    **Prevention:** stick to defaults (25 invites/day, 30 DMs/day), allow warm-up on new accounts, use active-hours windows that match your normal usage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email sender bounced messages">
    Some bounces are normal (bad addresses, full mailboxes). If your bounce rate is > 5%, something is off.

    **Common causes:**
    - Your domain has poor sending reputation → use a warmed-up domain
    - You have no SPF / DKIM / DMARC configured → set these up with your DNS provider
    - Your emails are hitting spam → check your Brand & Voice for spammy phrases (excessive caps, "guaranteed", "limited time only")
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="WhatsApp sender disconnected">
    WhatsApp's phone-linked session expired.

    **Fix:** Settings → Sender accounts → [WhatsApp account] → Reconnect. Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp mobile app.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Watchlist Agents

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Watchlist Agent is 'Active' but finding zero prospects">
    Filters are too strict.

    **Check:**
    - **Filtered accordion** — is Guhan finding people but rejecting them all? Reason column tells you why.
    - **Titles** — try broader ("Sales Leader" instead of "VP of Enterprise Sales")
    - **HQ country** — is it filtering out people who are in your target country but their company HQ is elsewhere?
    - **Signal** — pick "any" not "all" signal logic; loosen recency window

    **Debug:** flip your Watchlist Agent to WHO-only (no signals). If it now finds prospects, your signal is the problem. If still zero, your WHO filters are too tight.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Watchlist Agent finds too many prospects (500+/day)">
    Filters are too loose.

    **Options:**
    - Set a **daily prospect target** on the Watchlist Agent (e.g., 25/day). Guhan pauses when you hit it.
    - Add a stricter **signal**
    - Tighten **titles**, **industry**, or **company size**
    - Raise the **score threshold**
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Same person keeps showing up in filtered as 'already in workspace'">
    Guhan found this person via a prior sweep or a different Watchlist Agent. They're not being messaged twice — this is normal deduplication.

    Find them via **Prospects** page (search their name) — they're already in your List somewhere.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Prospects landing but the wrong seniority">
    Common cause: your title filter is missing seniority modifiers.

    "Sales Director" isn't the same as "Director of Sales" — LinkedIn matches them differently. Add the specific version you want.

    Auto-variants may include titles below your target seniority. Delete unwanted variant chips from the WHO panel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Outreach Agents

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Outreach Agent is 'Active' but no messages have gone out">
    **Common causes:**
    - **Empty audience** — your List has no prospects yet. Wait for the Watchlist Agent to land some.
    - **Outside sending window** — Guhan won't send at 3am. Wait for your active hours.
    - **Sender broken** — check Sender accounts for red/amber dots.
    - **Auto-enroll off** — prospects need manual approval. Check the Outreach Agent's Leads tab for unapproved prospects.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reply rate is 0%">
    After 100+ sends with zero replies:
    - **Read a sent message** — look at any prospect timeline. Does the message read like something YOU'd write? Or does it feel like an AI blast?
    - **Adjust your Brand & Voice** — tone might be off, or you might have too many "always include" phrases making messages feel scripted
    - **Check your sequence** — is the first message a hard pitch (bad) or a soft observation (good)?
    - **Check timing** — sending Monday 9am? Everyone else is too. Try Tuesday 2pm.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reply rate is high but no meetings">
    Replies but no bookings usually means:
    - Your CTA is unclear — messages don't ask for a call
    - Your CTA is too heavy — asking for 30 min on first touch is a lot
    - Prospects reply "not now" — try a longer nurture sequence
    - Meeting request classification is misfiring — check Inbox tasks for "interested" replies you might have missed
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Messages feel repetitive across prospects">
    Guhan personalizes but your BRAND VOICE + TEMPLATE combo might be over-constrained.

    - Remove "always include" phrases that turn into filler
    - Simplify the sequence brief — over-specific briefs produce over-similar outputs
    - Turn on **Fetch recent post** for DM steps → messages ground in each prospect's actual content
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Inbox + replies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Replies aren't showing up in my Inbox">
    **For LinkedIn:** Guhan gets webhook notifications from Unipile. There's a 1-5 minute delay typically. If nothing arrives after 15+ minutes, your Unipile webhook may be broken — contact support.

    **For email:** replies land within 30 seconds usually. If not, check your sender's Reauth status.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A prospect replied but Guhan didn't classify it">
    Very short replies ("k", "thx", "no") sometimes get skipped. They'll still show in the prospect timeline but might not create a task.

    Also: replies from non-prospects (colleagues cc'd, autoresponders) get filtered.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Guhan created a task but classified the reply wrong">
    Report it — 👎 on the classification. We use these for model improvement.

    In the meantime: click the task's kebab menu → **Reclassify** → pick the correct category.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Meetings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prospect can't book any time on my calendar">
    Your Google/Outlook calendar shows you as busy across the offered window. Check:
    - Working hours in Google/Outlook are correct
    - No all-day blocking events that shouldn't be there
    - Meeting duration + buffer in Guhan aren't too long (30 min meeting + 15 min buffer = need 45 min gap; if your calendar shows only 30 min gaps, no slot appears)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Meeting booked but not on my calendar">
    Sync delay from Google/Outlook, usually resolves in ~1 minute.

    If it's been longer:
    - Check your Guhan calendar connection — Settings → Calendars → [account] → status should be Active
    - Reauth the calendar
    - Meeting should appear on your calendar as soon as Guhan resyncs
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Calendly / Cal.com booking not tracked">
    Guhan uses UTM parameters (Calendly) or custom metadata (Cal.com) to track bookings. If the prospect clicks the link but the tracking gets stripped:
    - Some URL shorteners strip UTMs — check your Guhan-generated links
    - Cal.com's webhook may not be configured — Settings → Calendars → Cal.com → check webhook status
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Credit balance says 0 but I just got a fresh plan">
    Sync delay. Refresh the page. If still 0, check:
    - Billing anniversary — did the plan actually renew? Settings → Billing → next renewal date
    - Payment issue — did the card charge fail? Look for a payment-failed banner
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Charged twice for the same plan">
    Contact hello@guhan.ai with your invoice number. We'll refund one immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't upgrade — 'plan change failed'">
    Usually a payment method issue. Update your card in Settings → Billing → Payment methods, then retry the upgrade.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
