## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need Sales Navigator?">
    No. Personal LinkedIn works fine for both sending and discovery. Sales Navigator gets slightly higher search quality but isn't required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to start?">
    No — 7-day free trial requires no card. Guhan asks for payment on day 8.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I try Guhan with a personal Gmail?">
    Yes. Personal Gmail and Outlook accounts work. Google Workspace + Microsoft 365 also work (sometimes require admin approval).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long from signup to first message going out?">
    ~10 minutes for setup + ~10 minutes for the first prospect to land. First message sends fire during your active hours — if you set up outside your window, sends queue for the next opening.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I import my own list of prospects?">
    Yes — CSV upload on the Lists page. Provide name + LinkedIn URL (email optional). Guhan enriches from LinkedIn and treats them like any other prospect.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## LinkedIn safety

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will my LinkedIn account get restricted?">
    Guhan is built inside LinkedIn's published safe limits — 25 invites/day, 30 DMs/day, throttled with random delays, warmed up over 30 days for new accounts. Following the defaults, restriction is very rare.

    Aggressive settings (widened caps, minimal warm-up, narrow sending windows) increase risk. Guhan warns you when you push past safe zones.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my main LinkedIn account?">
    Yes — that's the recommended pattern. Guhan mimics your normal usage patterns. If you already send occasional messages from LinkedIn, adding Guhan on top is a natural extension.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I turn off warm-up?">
    Yes — Settings → Sender accounts → [account] → Skip warm-up. Only recommended for established accounts (30+ days, 150+ connections).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I get restricted anyway?">
    Guhan detects the restriction + pauses that sender + shows a banner. You'll need to appeal to LinkedIn OR wait it out. Once restored, click Reauth in Settings; Guhan resumes at reduced pace to rebuild reputation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Messages + personalization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Guhan use my brand voice for every message?">
    Yes. Every draft references your Brand & Voice settings (tone, language, always-include, never-say). If a message doesn't sound like you, edit your voice — every future message will match.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I write the exact message text myself?">
    Yes — set the step's brief to the exact wording you want. Guhan will still personalize per prospect (their name, company, situation) but respect your wording otherwise.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I want to review every single message before it sends?">
    On any Send step, turn on **Require approval**. Guhan drafts + creates an Approve task in your Inbox. Nothing sends until you click Approve.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Guhan write in a language other than English?">
    Yes. Set **Preferred language** in Brand & Voice. Supported: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified). More on request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is this different from templates + merge tags?">
    Templates + merge tags produce the same text with {{firstName}} swapped in. Guhan reads each person's profile + writes a fresh message. Same product, but the invite to a Series A founder in fintech will read differently than the invite to a Series B founder in devtools.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Prospects + discovery

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where does Guhan get prospects from?">
    LinkedIn (Classic search + Sales Navigator when available) + Crustdata (LinkedIn-enriched company + person database). We do NOT use scraped email databases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Guhan find people with company email addresses?">
    Yes — when a step needs to send email, Guhan looks up the prospect's company email (4 credits per reveal). Discovery-time reveal is optional.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a prospect's LinkedIn is private?">
    Guhan can't enrich private profiles fully but can still add them to your List if we have basic info. Messages sent to private profiles may have less personalization.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I block specific people or companies?">
    Yes — Settings → Do Not Contact. Block by email, LinkedIn URL, or domain. Enforced globally across all your agents.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Guhan found 500 prospects and I'm overwhelmed">
    Two options:
    - **Tighten filters** on the Watchlist Agent — narrower title / smaller company size / stricter signal
    - **Set a daily prospect target** — cap the max landing per day (say, 25) so you never wake up to a wall of prospects
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing + credits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What actually costs credits?">
    - Discovering a prospect (1 credit each)
    - Revealing an email (4 credits)
    - Revealing a phone (12 credits)
    - AI reply draft (1 credit)
    - Recent-post fetch (0.25 credits — opt-in)
    - Ask Guhan overflow (1 credit per 50k tokens over daily quota)

    Sending doesn't cost credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    Discovery pauses. Email + phone reveals pause. AI reply drafts pause. Sending still works (already free). Buy a pack or wait for the monthly reset.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do credits roll over?">
    - **Monthly grant** (from your plan) — doesn't roll over. Refills on your billing anniversary.
    - **Top-up packs** — carry over 12 months. Spent AFTER the monthly grant is depleted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Yes — full refund within 7 days of any invoice. Email hello@guhan.ai.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel?">
    Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Takes effect at end of current billing cycle. Your data stays for 30 days after cancellation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Teams

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I invite teammates?">
    Yes — Settings → Members → Invite. Pick a role. They get an email link + join your workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can teammates share sender accounts?">
    Each sender has one owner — the person whose LinkedIn/email/WhatsApp it is. As a Workspace Admin, you can reassign senders to teammates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can two people work on the same prospect?">
    Yes — inbox tasks route to the sender's owner by default; you can reassign anytime. All actions on a prospect show in the shared timeline.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I have separate workspaces for separate products?">
    Yes — an organization can have multiple workspaces. Each workspace has separate senders, prospects, billing. Switch via the workspace picker in the sidebar.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Meetings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Guhan support Cal.com and Calendly?">
    Yes — paste your booking URL in Settings → Calendars. Guhan uses it as the scheduling link. You still get all your Cal.com / Calendly features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Guhan book without me approving?">
    Not out-of-the-box. The prospect books their own time from the link Guhan sends them. If you want more automated booking, use Cal.com routing forms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a prospect books a meeting outside my working hours?">
    Your calendar defines available slots. If your Google Calendar shows you as busy at 3am, prospects can't book that. If your calendar is empty at 3am, they could — set working hours in your calendar OR in Guhan Settings → Meeting preferences.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
