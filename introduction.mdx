## What Guhan does for you

Think of Guhan as an outbound teammate who never sleeps.

- **Finds people worth reaching out to** — every day, Guhan searches LinkedIn for people who match the customer you sell to. It watches for buying signals like "just raised funding" or "posted about a topic you care about."
- **Writes each message personally** — no template blasts. Guhan reads each person's profile and writes a message that references their actual work.
- **Runs the follow-up sequence** — invite → wait → DM → wait → email. Whatever cadence you set, Guhan runs it for you across LinkedIn, email, and WhatsApp.
- **Only interrupts you for real replies** — when someone actually engages, Guhan drops a task in your Inbox. Meeting requests turn into calendar links automatically.

## Who Guhan is for

Founders and small sales teams selling to a specific type of customer.

If you can answer these three questions, Guhan will work for you:

1. **Who's your ideal customer?** ("VP of Sales at SaaS companies in the US")
2. **What triggers your outreach?** ("They raised a Series A" or "They engaged with a competitor")
3. **What do you say to them?** ("We help X do Y — here's the value")

## Why founders pick Guhan

- **You look like a full BDR team** while it's just you. One person, ten conversations per day, all personalized.
- **Zero list-buying** — Guhan finds people via real-time signals, not stale scraped databases.
- **LinkedIn-safe** — Guhan throttles daily volume to what LinkedIn is comfortable with, so your account doesn't get restricted.
- **Your voice, not a template** — Guhan learns how you talk (from your website + your own edits) and writes in that tone.
