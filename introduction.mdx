Give it your website URL. Guhan reads your site, learns what you sell and who buys it, then goes and finds real people showing buying signals right now — and starts a personal conversation with each one on the channels you choose.

You show up when there's a real reply.

## Who Guhan is for

Founders and small sales teams who sell to a specific kind of buyer.

If you can answer these three questions, Guhan will work for you:

1. **Who's your ideal customer?** ("VP of Sales at SaaS companies in the US")
2. **What tells you they're ready?** ("They raised a Series A" or "They posted about hiring SDRs")
3. **What's your pitch?** ("We help X do Y — here's the value")

To start, all you need is your **website URL**. Guhan reads your site and pre-fills everything else.
