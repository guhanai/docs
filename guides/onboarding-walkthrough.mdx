description: What each step of the setup wizard actually does.

When you sign up, Guhan walks you through 7 quick steps. Here's what each one is doing and what to think about at each.

## Step 1 — Connect LinkedIn

**What Guhan does:** takes you to LinkedIn's OAuth screen, gets a token to send + search on your behalf.

**What to think about:**
- Use a real, established LinkedIn account with 100+ connections (better deliverability)
- Sales Navigator works fine but isn't required
- Don't use a brand-new account — LinkedIn is suspicious of anything under 30 days old

Click Connect LinkedIn, sign in on LinkedIn's page, allow Guhan → you'll be back in Guhan in seconds.

## Step 2 — Confirm your brand

**What Guhan does:** reads your website + LinkedIn profile → drafts your brand summary.

You'll see a form pre-filled with:
- **Company name**
- **Industry** — pick from LinkedIn's taxonomy
- **What you sell** — a 1-3 sentence description of your product / service
- **Who buys it** — a short line about your ideal customer
- **Customer pain points** — 2-3 problems your customers have
- **Preferred tone** — professional, casual, direct, friendly, playful

Each field has an **✨ AI-generated** pill if Guhan drafted it. Read every line — edit anything that doesn't sound like you.

**Why this matters:** every message Guhan writes references this summary. Weak summary = weak messages.

<Tip>
  You can come back to this any time via **Brand & Voice** in the sidebar. Nothing you set here is locked.
</Tip>

## Step 3 — Titles

**What Guhan does:** asks who you sell to (their job titles) and where they live.

Add each target title as a chip:
- **VP of Sales**
- **Head of RevOps**
- **Director of Growth**

You don't need to add every variation. Guhan matches "VP of Sales" against "Vice President Sales", "SVP Sales", "Head of Revenue", etc. automatically.

For location:
- Enter cities, countries, or regions
- Autocomplete suggests options as you type
- Multiple = OR logic (US OR Canada OR UK)

## Step 4 — Firmographics

**What Guhan does:** narrows the search to specific company shapes.

- **Industry** — pick from the list; multiple OK
- **Company size** — headcount range slider (e.g., 50–500)
- **HQ country** — optional; useful for "US SaaS only" targeting

<Note>
  If you leave a field blank, Guhan doesn't filter on it. Leave industry blank if you sell to everyone.
</Note>

## Step 5 — Signals

**What Guhan does:** asks what event should trigger outreach.

Signal options are checkboxes. Common picks:

- ✅ **Funding raised recently** — great for founders selling to VC-backed startups
- ✅ **They're hiring** — great for tools targeting Sales, Engineering, Marketing hires
- ✅ **New leadership joined** — great for tools targeting decision-makers who need new stack
- ✅ **Engaged with a competitor's post** — great for evaluation-window catches
- ✅ **Posted about specific keywords** — great for topic-driven outreach

You can skip all of them → Guhan runs a plain ICP search.

<Warning>
  Pick at most 1-2 signals to start. More = narrower funnel, fewer prospects. You can add more later.
</Warning>

## Step 6 — Preview 5 prospects

**What Guhan does:** runs a REAL LinkedIn search using your filters and shows 5 people.

For each person:
- **Accept** — this is exactly the kind of person I want
- **Reject** — no, this isn't right

Guhan uses your accepts + rejects to tune the actual launch. If you reject 4 out of 5, Guhan will refine the search before it goes live.

<Tip>
  Look at their profile — their current role, their post activity, their headline. Would you send a message to this person? If yes, accept. If no, reject and note why.
</Tip>

## Step 7 — Launch

**What Guhan does:** creates the Watchlist Agent + Outreach Agent + activates them.

Behind the scenes:
- Your accepted preview prospects become the first prospects in your List (no waiting for the first sweep)
- A default sequence is created: **LinkedIn invite → wait 3 days → LinkedIn DM**
- The first message drafts begin generating
- Within 10-15 minutes, first sends start going out (during your active hours)

<Info>
  If it's outside your active sending window, sends queue for the next opening. Guhan never fires messages at 3am.
</Info>

## After launch

You land on your **Outreach Agent detail page**. Here you can:

- Watch prospects arriving in the list
- Rewrite the sequence
- Add more sender accounts (email, WhatsApp)
- Adjust the Watchlist Agent's filters via **Sources** tab
