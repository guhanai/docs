Every person Guhan finds is a **Prospect** — a real human tied to a real **Company**. Both are first-class records with detail pages.

## Prospect

A prospect is one person. Each prospect record has:

- **Name, title, headline** — pulled from their LinkedIn profile
- **Company** — links to the Company record
- **Contact channels** — LinkedIn URL, email (if revealed), phone (if revealed)
- **Source** — which Watchlist Agent found them + which signal triggered
- **Score** — how well they match your ICP (0–100)
- **Timeline** — every message sent to them + every reply received
- **Notes** — anything you want to jot down

Prospects live in **Lists**. A prospect can be in multiple Lists at once (though only one Outreach Agent will message them at a time).

## Company

A company is the org a prospect works for. Each company record has:

- **Name, domain, LinkedIn URL**
- **Industry, headcount, HQ location**
- **Description** — pulled from LinkedIn's company page
- **Signals** — recent funding, hiring activity, etc. (drives your Watchlist Agent)
- **Related prospects** — everyone you've discovered at this company

## Prospects page

The **Prospects** page shows everyone in your workspace:

- **Filter by** — List, Watchlist Agent, Outreach Agent, status, source, channel
- **Search by** — name, title, company, email
- **Sort by** — most recent, name, score
- **Export** — download as CSV (up to 10,000 at a time)

By default it shows **landed** prospects only — people who made it through Guhan's filtering. Toggle **Include filtered-out** to see everyone (including those Guhan decided didn't match).

## Companies page

The **Companies** page shows all orgs Guhan has discovered.

By default it shows companies with at least one landed prospect. Toggle **Include companies without prospects** to see everyone (including companies discovered via signal but nobody landed yet).

## Blocking

If you never want to reach out to a person or a company again:

- **Block a prospect** — the prospect and everyone at their company (if it's a competitor) can move to **Do Not Contact**
- **Block a company** — same effect, applied across all prospects at that company

Blocked = Guhan will never message them, even if they show up in a future Watchlist Agent sweep.
