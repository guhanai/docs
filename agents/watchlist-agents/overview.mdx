**A Watchlist Agent is your prospect finder. Tell it who your ideal customer is and what should make them worth reaching out to. It searches Guhan's data pipelines every day and drops matches in your List.**

You describe your ideal customer + the triggers that say "they're ready now". The agent does the searching — your personal accounts are never touched.

## What it does

Every day (or on a schedule you pick), the Watchlist Agent:

1. Searches Guhan's data pipelines for people matching your **ICP** (job title + location + industry + company size)
2. Filters that list by the **triggers** you set (funding, hiring, competitor engagement, etc.)
3. Adds qualifying people to a **List** — your bucket of active prospects
4. From that List, your Outreach Agent picks them up and starts messaging

## What you configure

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who — your ideal customer">
    - **Job titles** — one or many ("VP of Sales", "Head of RevOps")
    - **Location** — where the person lives (city, country, or region)
    - **Industry** — what industry their company is in ("SaaS", "Fintech")
    - **Company size** — headcount range (50–500 employees)
    - **HQ country** — where the company is headquartered (optional)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When — the trigger">
    Pick zero, one, or several:
    - 💰 They raised funding recently
    - 📈 They're hiring for a role you sell to
    - 👔 A new leader joined in a relevant seat
    - 🎯 They engaged with a competitor's post
    - 📢 They posted about topics you care about
    - 🎁 They engaged with a specific post URL you paste in

    No signals? Guhan runs a plain ICP search — just people matching your customer description.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where prospects land">
    You bind the Watchlist Agent to a **List**. Every prospect it finds gets added to that List. From there, your Outreach Agent picks them up.

    You can point multiple Watchlist Agents at the same List (e.g., one for "VPs of Sales" + another for "Directors of Sales" both feeding your "Enterprise Sales Leaders" List).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Daily cadence

You don't schedule sweeps manually. Guhan runs the Watchlist Agent on a rhythm that matches your daily prospect target — usually **once per day per signal**, spread across the day to look natural.

<Note>
  You can set a **daily prospect target** (e.g., "give me 25 new prospects per day"). Once the target is hit, Guhan pauses the agent for the day and picks up tomorrow.
</Note>

## Seeing what it found

Every Watchlist Agent has a **detail page** showing:

- **Today's sweeps** — how many prospects landed today, filtered by which signal
- **Sweep history** — the last 30 sweeps, each drillable into per-prospect detail
- **Lifetime totals** — unique prospects found + how many landed vs. got filtered out

Prospects that don't quite match get filtered out ("wrong seniority", "wrong location") and show up in the **Filtered** accordion so you can spot-check what got rejected.

## When to run multiple Watchlist Agents

One workspace can run many Watchlist Agents at once. Common patterns:

- **One per persona** — "VPs of Sales" + "Directors of Sales" + "RevOps Leaders" each feeding the same List
- **One per market** — "US SaaS" + "EU SaaS" as separate agents
- **One per trigger** — "Hiring signal" + "Funding signal" as two agents; different messaging in each Outreach Agent
